About Yora Candle

Small batch, by hand, without compromise.

Maison YoRa was founded on one principle: every candle should be as beautiful as it is clean-burning. Handcrafted in Toronto using 100% natural soy wax, premium phthalate-free fragrance oils, and lead-free cotton wicks, each candle is poured in small batches and finished by hand. From our signature floral sculptures to our luxury jar candles, every piece is thoughtfully created to bring warmth, elegance, and timeless luxury into your home.

Our Promise

  • 100% natural soy wax — never paraffin
  • Premium phthalate-free fragrance oils
  • Lead-free cotton wicks
  • Handcrafted in Toronto, Canada
  • Small-batch production for exceptional quality
  • Luxury gift-ready packaging
  • Elegant, clean-burning candles designed for everyday living and meaningful gifting
